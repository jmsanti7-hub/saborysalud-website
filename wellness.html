Wellness Hub

“Tu progreso no se mide en perfección, se mide en presencia.”

This hub gently guides you through three main areas: Nutrition, Movement, and Mental Wellness. The goal is sustainable habits that fit real life, not extremes.

Mini Reset: Breathe, Move, Reset

This short video is designed to feel like a reset — a moment to breathe, reconnect with your body, and move gently. Use it on a lunch break, between classes, or any time your energy dips.

Remember: wellness doesn’t have to be extreme to be effective. A few minutes of intentional movement and deep breathing can shift how you feel for the rest of the day.

Nutrition / Nutrición

We focus on balanced plates that still feel like home: protein for strength, colorful fruits and veggies for nutrients, and the arroz, habichuelas, plátanos, and sazón that remind you of your roots.

Movement / Movimiento

Movement should support your life, not punish your body. Walks, lifting, dancing en la sala, stretching before bed — todo cuenta.

Mental Wellness / Bienestar Mental

Rest, boundaries, and self-compassion are part of wellness. You don’t “ruin” progress with one meal, one day, or one setback. Eres digna de cuidar tu cuerpo y tu mente tal como estás hoy.
