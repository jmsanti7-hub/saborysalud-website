Workout Plan

“Discipline and consistency build the version of you that you dream about.”

This 4-day split focuses on functional strength, conditioning, and mobility. Adjust sets and reps based on your experience and energy.

Day Focus Example Exercises
Day 1 Lower Body + Core Squats, reverse lunges, glute bridges, calf raises, planks
Day 2 Upper Body Push/Pull Push-ups, rows, shoulder presses, bicep curls, tricep dips
Day 3 Lower Body Power + Mobility Deadlifts, step-ups, lateral lunges, hip flexor stretches
Day 4 Conditioning + Core Intervals (walk/jog), mountain climbers, dead bugs, bird dogs

Track Your Progress

Use this space to log your wins: how you moved, how you ate, and how you felt. Even one sentence a day counts.

You can create a simple table or bullet list in your notebook, or build your own progress tracker later with JavaScript and localStorage as you advance in your coding skills.
