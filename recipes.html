Desayuno Boricua Power Bowl

Breakfast • High protein • Inspired by arroz con habichuelas

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 cup cooked rice and beans
  • 1 large egg, fried sunny-side up
  • 4–5 slices pan-fried sweet plantain (maduros)
  • 3–4 slices grilled queso fresco or halloumi-style cheese
  • ½ small avocado, sliced
  • 2–3 tablespoons pico de gallo or salsa fresca
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lime

Directions

  1. Warm the rice and beans and place them at the bottom of a bowl.
  2. Pan-fry the sweet plantain slices until golden on both sides; set aside.
  3. Grill or pan-sear the cheese until the outside is browned and crisp.
  4. Fry the egg sunny-side up and season lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble the bowl: rice and beans on the bottom, egg in the center, avocado, plantains, grilled cheese, and pico de gallo around the sides.
  6. Finish with a drizzle of olive oil, more salt and pepper if needed, and a squeeze of lime. Serve warm.

Lighter Arroz con Pollo Bowl

Lunch or Dinner • High protein • Flavor-forward

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 8 oz chicken breast, cut into small cubes
  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • ½ cup peas or mixed veggies
  • ½ small onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sofrito (or your favorite seasoning blend)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and lime for serving

Directions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken, season with salt, pepper, and sofrito; cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Stir in cooked rice and peas, mixing everything until warm and coated in flavor.
  4. Serve in bowls and top with cilantro and a squeeze of lime.

Tropical Sazón Protein Shake

Snack • Post-workout • Puerto Rican–inspired

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 scoop vanilla or unflavored protein powder
  • ½ cup frozen mango
  • ½ cup frozen pineapple
  • ½ small banana
  • 1 tablespoon oats
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed or chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • Optional: pinch of cinnamon and a drop of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more milk if needed.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy cold after your workout.
