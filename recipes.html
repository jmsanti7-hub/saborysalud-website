Desayuno Boricua Power Bowl

Breakfast • High protein • Inspired by gallo pinto / arroz y habichuelas

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice and black beans
  • 1 whole egg + 2 egg whites
  • 3–4 slices baked or air-fried sweet plantain (maduros)
  • ¼ cup grilled queso fresco or panela (or halloumi-style)
  • ½ small avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp pico de gallo or salsa
  • 1 tsp olive oil, salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lime

Directions

  1. Warm the rice and beans and place them at the bottom of a bowl.
  2. Cook the egg and egg whites scrambled or over-easy; add on top.
  3. Grill or pan-sear the cheese until golden on both sides.
  4. Add avocado, plantain, and pico de gallo around the bowl.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and lime. Serve warm.

Lighter Arroz con Pollo Bowl

Lunch or Dinner • High protein • Flavor-forward

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 8 oz chicken breast, cut into small cubes
  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • ½ cup peas or mixed veggies
  • ½ small onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp sofrito (or your favorite seasoning blend)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro and lime for serving

Directions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken, season with salt, pepper, and sofrito; cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Stir in cooked rice and peas, mixing everything until warm and coated in flavor.
  4. Serve in bowls, top with fresh cilantro and lime juice.

Tropical Sazón Protein Shake

Snack • Post-workout • Puerto Rican–inspired

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 scoop vanilla or unflavored protein powder
  • ½ cup frozen mango
  • ½ cup frozen pineapple
  • ½ small banana
  • 1 tbsp oats
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed or chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • Optional: pinch of cinnamon and a drop of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more milk if needed.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy cold after your workout.
