High-Protein Arroz con Todo Breakfast Bowl

Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice and beans
  • 1 egg, fried or over-easy
  • 3–4 slices of grilled cheese (halloumi or queso fresco-style)
  • ½ small avocado, sliced
  • ¼ cup pico de gallo or salsa
  • 5–6 slices of sweet plantain (maduros)
  • Salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil

Directions

  1. Warm the rice and beans and add them to a bowl.
  2. Pan-fry the plantains and cheese until golden.
  3. Cook the egg to your liking and place it on top of the rice.
  4. Add avocado slices and pico de gallo to the side.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and a light drizzle of olive oil. Enjoy warm.

Crispy Tostones with Chunky Guac

Serves: 2–3 as a snack

Ingredients

  • 2 green plantains, peeled and sliced into rounds
  • 1–2 tablespoons avocado or olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • ¼ cup diced tomato
  • 2 tablespoons diced red onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • Juice of ½ lime
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Pan-fry plantain slices in a little oil until lightly golden, then flatten with a cup or tostonera.
  2. Return them to the pan and cook until crispy. Sprinkle with salt.
  3. Mash the avocados in a bowl and stir in tomato, onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Serve the tostones hot with a generous scoop of guac on top.
