Protein Café Smoothie

A coffee-based smoothie for busy mornings when you want caffeine, protein, and something cold and easy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cooled brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup milk or milk alternative
  • 1 scoop vanilla or chocolate protein powder
  • 1 small frozen banana (or 1/2 for less sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter or almond butter
  • 3–5 ice cubes (optional, for thickness)

Directions

  1. Add coffee, milk, protein powder, banana, and nut butter to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth. Add ice if you want it thicker and blend again.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy as a breakfast or snack.

High-Protein Arroz con Pollo Bowl

A simplified, weeknight-friendly version of a comforting arroz con pollo–style bowl.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown or white rice
  • 4–5 oz cooked chicken breast or chicken thigh, chopped
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup sautéed peppers and onions
  • 1/4 cup corn (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1–2 tablespoons salsa or pico de gallo
  • 1 tablespoon olive or avocado oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, and cumin to taste
  • Fresh lime and cilantro (optional)

Directions

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add the oil.
  2. Add peppers and onions and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the chicken, black beans, corn, and seasoning. Warm through.
  4. Place the cooked rice in a bowl.
  5. Top the rice with the chicken and veggie mixture.
  6. Add salsa or pico, lime juice, and cilantro if desired.

High-Protein Carb-Conscious Breakfast Plate

A balanced breakfast with protein, fiber, and a moderate amount of carbs to keep you full and energized.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs (or 3 egg whites + 1 whole egg)
  • 2–3 tablespoons shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sautéed spinach or mixed greens
  • 1/2 small sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste
  • Optional: sliced avocado or a small piece of fruit on the side

Directions

  1. Heat a small pan over medium heat and add the olive oil.
  2. Add the cubed sweet potato and cook until tender and lightly browned.
  3. In another pan, scramble or fry the eggs to your liking. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Quickly sauté the spinach or greens in the same pan until wilted.
  5. Assemble your plate: sweet potato, eggs, and greens together.
  6. Add cheese and avocado if using. Enjoy warm.
